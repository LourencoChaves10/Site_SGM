About the System

SGM (Sistema de Gestão Mecânica) is a modern digital platform developed to optimize, organize, and streamline the daily operations of automotive repair workshops. The system was designed to improve efficiency, transparency, and communication between the workshop and its customers. With an advanced and user-friendly technological interface, SGM allows customers to easily monitor the progress of their vehicle repairs in real time. Through the platform, clients can check the repair status, receive updates, and stay informed about the work being carried out on their vehicles, creating a more transparent and trustworthy service experience. SGM provides a complete management solution for workshops by integrating several essential functions into a single system. These include customer management, vehicle registration, repair tracking, service history, and work order organization. By centralizing all this information, the system helps reduce paperwork, prevent errors, and improve the overall workflow of the workshop. The platform was designed with simplicity and usability in mind. Its intuitive interface allows mechanics, administrative staff, and workshop managers to easily access and manage information without requiring advanced technical knowledge. This enables staff to focus more on delivering high-quality mechanical services while the system efficiently handles the administrative and organizational tasks. In addition, SGM helps workshops improve productivity, maintain organized records of repairs and customers, and provide a more professional service. By adopting digital management tools like SGM, mechanical workshops can modernize their operations, enhance customer satisfaction, and remain competitive in an increasingly technology-driven industry.

System Features

Client Management

Register and manage customer information and service history.

Vehicle Tracking

Monitor repair status and maintenance schedules.

Manage stock levels and receive alerts for low inventory.

Invoice Generation

Create invoices and generate automatic service reports.

Role-Based Access

Different access levels for admin, mechanics and secretary.

Reports & Analytics

View monthly performance statistics and financial reports.
